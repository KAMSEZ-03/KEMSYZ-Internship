## Overview

During Week 4 of my internship at CODELOUNGE Pvt. Ltd., I focused on ideation and pre-development planning for the **E-Learn** project.  
These prototypes were created to visualize user flows, validate ideas, and explore potential UI structures before starting full development.

They helped in:
- Understanding user navigation
- Planning feature hierarchy
- Visualizing educator and student workflows
- Structuring the app architecture
